Frequently Asked Questions

How do I place an order?

You can place an order by calling our pizzeria at +31 6 29906406 or by visiting our website and using our online ordering system.

Yes, we offer delivery services within a 5-mile radius. Delivery charges may apply.

We accept cash, credit cards, ideal, and mobile payment methods like Apple Pay and Google Pay.

Absolutely! You can customize your pizza with a variety of toppings when placing your order.

Yes, we offer gluten-free crust and vegan cheese options for our pizzas.
